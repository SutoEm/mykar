# User Personas

## Persona 1: Careful Owner

Profile: Owns one personal vehicle and wants to avoid forgetting maintenance, insurance, inspection, and document dates.

Needs:

- Simple vehicle profile
- Maintenance history
- Document storage
- Reminders
- Clear next actions

Risks:

- Leaves if setup is too long
- Does not trust unclear AI answers
- May not know technical vehicle terms

Success Moment:

They add the vehicle and see what information is missing without feeling overwhelmed.

## Persona 2: Used-Car Seller

Profile: Wants to prove the vehicle was cared for before selling it.

Needs:

- Clean vehicle history
- Documents and maintenance timeline
- Exportable proof later
- Confidence that private data is not exposed accidentally

Risks:

- May only return near sale time
- Needs a clear reason to keep records early

Success Moment:

They can show a structured maintenance memory instead of scattered receipts and photos.

## Persona 3: New Vehicle Buyer

Profile: Bought a used vehicle and wants to understand what is known, missing, and risky.

Needs:

- Ownership start point
- Missing document detection
- Maintenance baseline
- Reminder setup

Risks:

- Has incomplete history
- May blame the product for missing data

Success Moment:

They understand the current state of the vehicle and what to do first.

## Persona 4: Family Vehicle Manager

Profile: Manages more than one vehicle for a household.

Needs:

- Garage overview
- Multiple vehicles
- Due reminders
- Simple record entry

Risks:

- Needs a higher-information dashboard later
- May require shared access in a future phase

Success Moment:

They can see which vehicle needs attention without opening each record.
