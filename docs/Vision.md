# Vision

My Kar is the AI-powered digital memory and identity layer for vehicles.

The first product should help owners reliably understand and preserve a vehicle's story. Over time, the platform can connect maintenance history, ownership context, documents, diagnostics, marketplace activity, and service interactions into one trusted vehicle identity.

## Product Promise

My Kar helps a vehicle owner answer three questions with confidence:

- What do I own?
- What has happened to it?
- What should I do next?

## Long-Term Direction

The long-term product should become the trusted memory layer for private vehicle ownership. It should support individual owners first, then expand into service providers, marketplaces, insurers, fleet workflows, and AI-assisted maintenance intelligence.

## Product Principles

- Trust before automation.
- Vehicle memory before generic AI chat.
- Simple owner workflows before marketplace expansion.
- Documentation before implementation.
- Human approval before AI-driven changes.

## Non-Goals

- My Kar is not a social network.
- My Kar is not a generic chatbot.
- My Kar is not a marketplace in the first product cycle.
- My Kar should not depend on direct mobile-to-database coupling.
