# Manifesto

Vehicles have memory, but most owners lose it.

Maintenance receipts disappear. Photos stay in camera rolls. Inspection dates are forgotten. Ownership context lives in chats, paper folders, and memory. When a vehicle changes hands, most of its story disappears.

My Kar exists to make vehicle ownership clearer, safer, and more trustworthy.

## Beliefs

- A vehicle is more than a plate number and model year.
- The history of a vehicle should be useful to its current owner.
- Trustworthy data is more valuable than flashy automation.
- AI should explain and assist, not pretend to know what was never recorded.
- The first product should be simple enough to use repeatedly.

## Product Culture

We will build in phases.

We will document before implementing.

We will reject premature scope.

We will protect users from losing important vehicle information.

We will not ship complexity just because the idea sounds impressive.

## Operating Rule

If a feature does not make the current phase stronger, it belongs in `Graveyard.md` or a later phase.
